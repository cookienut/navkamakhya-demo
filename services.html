Our Specialization

Crew Management

Navkamakhya is a ship management company, and crew management is the core service we specialize in — end-to-end manning support, from sourcing a candidate to keeping their documentation current for the length of their contract.

Overview

A single point of accountability for manning

Shipowners and operators lose time when crewing is split across multiple uncoordinated vendors. Navkamakhya's crew management service is structured so that one team is accountable for a seafarer from first contact through to sign-off — reducing handoff errors and keeping principals informed at each stage.

Scope of Service

What our Crew Management covers

  • 1

    Recruitment & Manning

    Sourcing and screening seafarers — officers and ratings — against the rank, certification and experience profile a vessel requires, and maintaining a bench of pre-verified candidates for faster turnaround on crew changes.

  • 2

    Documentation & Certification

    Verifying COC/COP, STCW, medical and other statutory certificates against flag-state and vessel-trade requirements before a candidate is proposed, and tracking renewal dates so certificates don't lapse mid-contract.

  • 3

    Flag Endorsement Coordination

    Coordinating flag-state endorsements and certificate equivalency documentation required for a seafarer to serve on a specific flag of registry.

  • 4

    Payroll Coordination

    Coordinating wage processing in line with the seafarer's contract (CBA/MLC-aligned), so payments reach seafarers accurately and on schedule.

  • 5

    Training Liaison

    Tracking mandatory and vessel-specific training requirements, and coordinating with approved training institutes ahead of sign-on.

  • 6

    Travel & Sign-On Logistics

    Arranging travel, visas and local transit for embarkation and disembarkation, so a seafarer joins the vessel on schedule and properly briefed.

  • 7

    Seafarer Welfare Support

    A point of contact for seafarers and their families while onboard — covering routine queries, contract clarifications and support during medical or personal emergencies.

Why It Matters

Crewing is a compliance function, not just a staffing one

Fewer PSC findings

Certificate and documentation checks completed before sign-on reduce the risk of port state control detentions tied to crew documentation.

Faster crew changes

A pre-screened candidate bench means urgent replacements can be actioned in hours rather than days.

One accountable partner

A single crewing partner, rather than fragmented vendors, means fewer handoff errors and clearer accountability.
